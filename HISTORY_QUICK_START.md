# Quick Start Guide - CELTECH History with Text-to-Speech

## 🚀 How to Access

1. **Open the Application**
   - Navigate to: `c:\ai\school-tts\index.html`
   - Or double-click the file in File Explorer

2. **Go to History Tab**
   - Click the **"History"** tab (🏛️ icon) in the navigation bar
   - You'll see 15 comprehensive history entries

## 🎧 Using Text-to-Speech

### Method 1: Click to Listen
1. Click on any history card
2. The TTS panel opens automatically
3. Click the **Play** button (▶️) to start listening

### Method 2: Quick Listen
1. Click the **"Listen"** button on any card
2. Playback starts immediately

### TTS Controls
- **Play/Pause** (▶️/⏸️): Start or pause playback
- **Stop** (⏹️): Stop and reset
- **Speed Slider**: Adjust from 0.5x to 2.0x
- **Voice Selector**: Choose different voices
- **Close** (✕): Close the TTS panel

## 🔍 Searching History

### Text Search
1. Type in the search bar at the top
2. Try keywords like:
   - "maritime" - Find maritime programs
   - "Dragonpay" - Online payment info
   - "credo" - College philosophy
   - "1959" - Founding history
   - "facilities" - Campus resources

### Voice Search
1. Click the microphone icon (🎤)
2. Speak your search query
3. Results appear automatically

## 💡 Tips

- **Keyboard Shortcuts**:
  - `Space`: Play/Pause (when TTS panel is open)
  - `Esc`: Close TTS panel

- **Best Experience**:
  - Use Chrome or Edge for full voice search support
  - Enable microphone permission for voice search
  - Adjust speed to your preference (1.0x is normal)

- **Dark Mode**:
  - Click the sun/moon icon (☀️/🌙) in the header
  - Preference is saved automatically
