📱 How to Use

  1. Browse Categories: Tap on Announcements, Events, Schedules, or News tabs
  2. Select Content: Click on any card to view the full information
  3. Listen: Press the "Listen" button to activate text-to-speech
  4. Control Playback: Use Play/Pause and Stop buttons
  5. Customize: Adjust speed (0.5x - 2.0x) and select different voices
  6. Toggle Theme: Click the sun/moon icon for dark/light mode

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space - Play/Pause speech
  • Esc - Close TTS panel

📚 Content Categories

  • 📢 Announcements: Important school notices and updates
  • 📅 Events: Upcoming activities and competitions
  • ⏰ Schedules: Class times, exams, and regular activities
  • 📰 News: School achievements and latest news

🎯 Priority System

  • 🔴 High Priority: Critical announcements requiring immediate attention
  • 🟡 Medium Priority: Important notices to be aware of
  • 🟢 Low Priority: General information and updates
💡 Pro Tip: Add SchoolVoice to your home screen for quick access! On mobile browsers, use "Add to Home Screen" from the browser menu.
🎧 Best Experience: Use headphones or earphones for better audio quality, especially in noisy environments.

🔧 Browser Compatibility

  • ✅ Chrome/Edge: Recommended (best voice quality)
  • ✅ Safari: Excellent on iOS devices
  • ✅ Firefox: Good support
  • ✅ Opera: Full support

❓ Troubleshooting

  • No sound? Check device volume and unmute your browser
  • Voice not working? Try a different browser or voice option
  • Slow performance? Close other browser tabs
  • Theme not saving? Enable cookies in browser settings
